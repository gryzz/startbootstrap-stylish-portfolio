Useful Information

Weather

Autumn in the Netherlands can be quite cold and rainy. So, don't forget your coat and umbrella. Weather you can check on these sites:

  • www.weather-forecast.com
  • www.bbc.com/weather
  • www.wunderground.com


Transportation

Transportation will be arranged between venues where our wedding will take place. You have to get to celebration and from celebration to your place by your own. For public transport you can check following websites:

  • www.9292.nl - source of travel information for public transport. They bring all information from all transport companies together. Here you can check how to get from point A to point B.
  • www.ns.nl - railways site, where you can check trains timetable and journey costs.
  • www.utc.nl - Utrecht taxi
You can find bus stop near Utrecht Central Station, check map. From there you can get to the church where wedding celebration takes start.


Where to eat?

If you located near Utrecht Central Station, you can find a lot of cafe/bistro in station shopping mall. Also you can find variety of restaurants in Utrecht Center.
